# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, constructive, patient, and focused on the project. Accept responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and respect reasonable boundaries. Harassment, threats, sexualized attention, discriminatory language, doxxing, and sustained disruption are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns confidentially through [GitHub private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/sageata/dita-editor/security/advisories/new). The project owner will review reports promptly, protect reporter privacy as far as practical, and may edit, reject, or remove contributions or temporarily or permanently ban participation. False or retaliatory reports are also unacceptable.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone officially represents the project. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately, and attentive to safety.

This code is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
